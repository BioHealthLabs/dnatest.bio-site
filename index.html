What is a DNA test?

A consumer DNA test analyzes genetic markers to estimate ancestry, identify relatives, and sometimes provide limited health or trait information. These tests are informational, not diagnostic.

What DNA tests are good at

  • Finding biological relatives
  • Estimating ancestry regions
  • Providing genetic traits and lifestyle insights

What DNA tests cannot guarantee

  • Exact ethnicity percentages
  • Medical diagnoses
  • Complete answers for underrepresented populations

Types of DNA tests

DNA tests generally fall into ancestry tests, health-focused tests, or combined kits. Each serves different goals.
